Open-source platform for anonymous civic participation in Greece.

Vote on real Hellenic Parliament bills anonymously after a Greek-number HLR network-status check. HLR does not prove SIM possession or identity. Your phone number is never stored — only a protected cryptographic nullifier is kept.

Features:
• Vote YES/NO/ABSTAIN on real parliamentary bills
• Political Compass (48 questions, computed locally)
• Divergence Index: citizen votes vs parliament decisions
• 325 municipalities and 13 regions covered
• Full anonymity via Ed25519 cryptography
• No accounts, no email, no cookies

This is NOT a government app. Votes are informational only.

Data sources: hellenicparliament.gr, diavgeia.gov.gr
