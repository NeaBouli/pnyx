ekklesia is an independent, open-source direct democracy platform for Greek citizens.

Vote on real parliamentary bills, regional and municipal decisions — anonymously and securely.

Features:
• Citizen Voting — Vote Yes/No/Abstain on real bills from the Hellenic Parliament
• Divergence Score — See how Parliament votes vs. citizens
• Party Comparison — Which party agrees most with the people?
• Municipal Governance — Diavgeia integration for regional decisions
• AI Summaries — Bill analysis powered by Ollama + DeepL
• Full Anonymity — Ed25519 cryptographic signatures, nullifier hashes, zero PII

Security:
• No personal data stored — phone number deleted after verification
• Private key stays on your device — never leaves your phone
• Every vote is cryptographically signed — impossible to falsify
• Results archived on Arweave — permanent, immutable audit trail

This platform is not affiliated with any government entity. All votes are informational only and carry no legal binding force.

Data sources: Hellenic Parliament, Diavgeia (diavgeia.gov.gr), data.gov.gr
